# Simplified Spatial Sentiment Analysis Assignment

## Overview

For this assignment, you will complete a "light" version of the analysis done by the rest of the class. You will write an essay answering the following research question:

> **How do JMU and GMU students speak differently about the place where they live and study?**

To support your answer, you will use visualizations created in **Voyant Tools** and **Google Sheets**.

---

## Instructions

Using the two provided datasets (JMU and GMU), write an essay of approximately **900-1200 words** that includes the following components:

---

### 1. Introduction (1 Paragraph)

Provide a general overview of your research and summarize your main findings.

---
